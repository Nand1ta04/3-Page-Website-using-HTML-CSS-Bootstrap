Analysis 2: Highest-Lowest Sales by Category




As we observe this graph we conclude that cookies have had the most sales in 2022 which is 8 395,78$. The lowest sales in 2022 is snacks which is 1 342.02$. The total sales for 2022 was 17 988.66$.





In the next year, we could see that both the total sales of bars and cookies have increased while the total sales of crackers and snacks have reduced. Total sales in cookies still remains as the highest sales out of all of the food categories with 8816.63$. This year crackers have the lowest total sales which is 837.60$. The total sales for 2023 was 15 336.92$.
