Food Analysis in the USA


BarChart Palate aims to analyze the different food consumptions in the USA of snacks, cookies, bars, and crackers.


Who We Are


We are three management students studying at Mcgill. This website created by us shows a variety of graphs containing data of the different food consumptions from 4 locations in the USA such as Boston, Los Angeles, San Diego, and New York from the years 2022 & 2023. In which we discuss the quantity demanded by location and highest-lowest sales by product.
